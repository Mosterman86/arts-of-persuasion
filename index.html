Something to consider
When you leave the museum today, think about how other forms of media and communication you encounter attempt to persuade you.
Transfer or Association Linking a person, product, or idea with something that already has positive or negative connotations in the audience's mind. The goal is to transfer those existing feelings and associations to the new target.
Bandwagon A method that encourages people to adopt a belief or behavior because "everyone else is doing it." It exploits the human tendency to conform and follow the crowd.
Identification / Plain Folks Used to convince the audience that ideas are good because they come from common, everyday people. Note the use of what would have been considered a 'common' citizen at the time, though this representation would not be considered inclusive or representative of many groups today.
United We Win, 1942
Photographer: Howard Liberman
Publisher: War Manpower Commission
Printer: U.S. Government Printing Office, Washington, D.C.
